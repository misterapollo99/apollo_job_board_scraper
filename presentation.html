Challenge #1: Domain Resolution

The Problem

  • Job boards give: "Flexxter GmbH"
  • Apollo needs: "flexxter.com"
  • No direct mapping available

My Solution - 3-Tier Strategy

1

Apollo Organization Search

POST /organizations/search with company name

‚úì SUCCESS ‚Üí Use returned domain ‚úó FAIL ‚Üí Try Tier 2
2

Smart Domain Guessing

Remove: "GmbH", "Inc", "LLC", "Corp"

Clean: "Flexxter"

Try: flexxter.com, flexxter.io, flexxter.ai

For each guess ‚Üí Try Tier 3
3

Validation via Enrichment

POST /organizations/enrich with guessed domain

Compare: returned company name vs original

‚úì MATCH ‚Üí Valid domain ‚úó MISMATCH ‚Üí Reject, try next guess
"Without validation, I was getting Google's data for every query. Validation catches bad domain matches."

Credit Efficiency: User Control

The Credit Problem

  • Email enrichment: 1 credit
  • Phone enrichment: 8 credits
  • 35 contacts √ó 9 credits = 315 credits per company!

My Solution: Granular Control

Search (Low/No Credits)

  • Find 35 contacts
  • Name, Title, LinkedIn
  • Seniority, Department
  • Company context

Enrichment (1-8 Credits Each)

  • User reviews contacts
  • Clicks "Get Email" for CEO
  • Clicks "Get Phone" for VP CS
  • Skips 33 other contacts
Result: 1 + 8 = 9 credits used (vs 315 if auto-enriched all)
"Why pay for 35 contacts when you'll only reach out to 3? Give users control."
